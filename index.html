Our Mission

Giving.org Kenya is dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged communities through sustainable projects in education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. We believe in local solutions and collaborative action.

Our Key Projects

Education Access

Providing resources, scholarships, and infrastructure for better learning environments.

Health & Wellness

Running mobile clinics and health awareness programs in remote areas.

Empowerment

Offering vocational training and small business support for sustainable livelihoods.
